TiiQu Suitability Match

Empowering smart people to carry out smart contracts.

Welcome to TiiQu's suitability match system. Here, we have a very precise agenda. The creation of a trustless global platform where employee-employer collaborations are facilitated via smart contracts. For skill professionals, this means unbounded opportunities for career growth, made possible through a state-of-the-art algorithm that will provide customized recommendations of jobs that best match their skills and interests. For employers, the TiiQu suitability algorithm will guarantee access to the liquid workforce needed to succeed in a fast changing tech world. The present version of the algorithm is a simple proof of concept, allowing an employer to search for employees that meet a desired Trust Quotien (TQ) requirement and a minimum number of skills.
